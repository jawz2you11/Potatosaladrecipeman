Potato Salad Recipe

Creamy yummy potato salad that is quick and easy to make

Ingredients

  • Potatos: Russets, Yukon gold, Baking
  • Seasonings: Iodine Salt, Pepper
  • Vegatables: Sweet Red Onions, Celery,
  • Herbs: Dill & Parsley
  • Creams: Mayo, Sour Cream, Mustard, Vinegar(apple cider)

    • Amounts per

    • 2 Pounds(907g) Potatoes
    • 1 Tablespoon Apple Cider vinegar
    • 1/2 Cup(113g) Sour cream
    • 1/4 cup(56g) mayonnaise
    • 1 Tablespoon yellow mustard
    • 1/2 medium red onion, finely chopped(not minced)
    • 3 celery stalks, chopped to about 1/2 cup
    • 1 pickle, finely chopped, 1/3 cup
    • Optional: 2 hardboiled eggs, peeled and chopped
    • 1/4 cup chopped parsley dill, and cilantro
    • 2tbsp Salt and Pepper

Instructions

  1. Cook potatoes in salted(1tbsp) water for taste -Between 10-20mins on moderate heat.
  2. Pour vinegar over potatoes for flavor
  3. Prepare ice bath: large bowl, 1 cup of ice, 1/2cup water
  4. Chill potatoes
  5. Peel Potatoes and chop into smaller pieces
  6. In a seperate bowl:Mix together seasoning sour cream, mayonnaise, and mustard
  7. Add everything together now, dont mash the potatoes!
  8. Season with salt and pepper
  9. Refrigerate for 30 minutes to let flavor improve.
  10. Serve and ENJOY!!
